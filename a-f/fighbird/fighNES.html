Taiyou no Yuusha Fighbird

  • Company: Irem
  • Date Released: 1992
  • Genre: Shooting game
    Controls
  • A button: Select Machine
  • B button: Shoot
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]
You collect various power-ups along the way. There are also two different characters you can choose. During the game, you can choose different machines to use. Each machine has its own unique shooting capacity.
