This is a horizontal scrolling game where you control one of the five Car Rangers. If you recognize them, it is because Saban is currently hacking this show into his Mighty Morphin Power Rangers timeline to keep that cash cow producing. You go along defeating enemies and collecting parts. At certain points, there are platforms where you pushing Up will take you to a place to obtain items or parts. By punching vehicles and platforms with a question mark, you can collect parts as well as weapons. Haven't played it long enough, but there is a gauge that fills up when you collect 60 parts. I am assuming you get a huge power-up when you fill the entire gauge.

SD Ultraman Battle 7

    Controls
  • A button: Jump
  • B button: Kick
  • X button: Charge Power
  • Y button: Punch
  • L button: [not used]
  • R button: Block
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]
This is a unique fighting game. You fight Street Fighter style in an area that allows you to move in any direction like in Final Fight. By charging your power, you can unleash a super blast at your opponent. The blast you unleash is chosen from a menu that pops up. While the blast is hitting your opponent, tapping a button will increase the damage done by the blast. I am still unsure how to unleash or block these super blasts.
