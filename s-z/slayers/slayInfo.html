Description:

  After a successful raid, a bandit group is celebrating how much  
  booty they got.  In the middle of the dancing and bonfire, a     
  Fireball (TM) comes streaking out of the sky.  The fortress wall 
  is blown inward and major destruction follows.  In the middle of 
  all of this is a small unassuming woman, who the bandit leader   
  recognizes as "Li-Li-Lina Inverse!".  After dispatching the rest 
  of the bandits, Lina helps herself to the booty.  The next day,  
  Lina is accosted by another bandit group and finds herself being 
  'rescued' by Gourry, a dim-witted, but earnest warrior.  Gourry  
  rapidly finds out that this 'little girl' is a great magician    
  and fighter with a reputation to match.  Not to mention her      
  enormous appetite.  This is a series that makes fun of virtually 
  every element of RPGs that it can find, with a little serious    
  adventure mixed in.
