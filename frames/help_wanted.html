Help Wanted!

I would like to keep this site as complete as possible, but I am going to need help to do this. There are many items with incomplete or vague descriptions that I just don't have the time or resources to complete. Below are lists of the items I need more information on grouped by gaming system. If you can provide any information on any of these items, please email me the information. If you have a web page with the information, I would be more than happy to link to it.

So why should you help me? You would be helping out your fellow anime video game players as well. But, you also get special Contributor status in my book. This means more than just a nod in the Acknowledgements section. I cannot go into full detail here, but I can assure you that I would make it well worth your time and effort if you did contribute some information (let's just say that you would never have to visit the Emulation section very frequently).
