Godzilla

  • Genre: War strategy/Shooting game
    Controls
  • A button: Select Monster/Punch
  • B button: Unselect Monster/Kick
  • Start: Shoot
  • Select: Pause
You control Godzilla and Mothra as they move around a grid to gain position on the advancing enemies. Moving from square to square can cause you to enter a shooting stage where you must destroy the enemy guns, ships, and other objects. No other information available at this time.

Godzilla 2

  • Genre: War strategy game
This time around you control the military as they try to stop Godzilla and other creatures from destroying the city. No other information available at this time.
