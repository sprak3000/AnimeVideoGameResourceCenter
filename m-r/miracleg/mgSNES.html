From Lasse Reinikainen:

Instead of killing enemies, you throw candy at them. They eat the candy and are stunned while they chew it. If you are looking for an overly cute game, this is the one to pick up. At certain parts, your sister will tell you the color of the platform you must jump onto. If you jump onto the wrong platform, you lose a life.
