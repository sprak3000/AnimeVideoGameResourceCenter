Description:

  Yohko is an ordinary schoolgirl who is infatuated with one of    
  her attractive male schoolmates.  What she *doesn't* know        
  is that she comes from a family of demon hunters...and she is    
  next in line to receive the power.  A powerful demon is aware    
  of this, and seeks to kill her, or prevent her from receiving    
  the power.  A light-hearted romp into the demon-hunting/cute     
  girl anime stereotype.  The 2nd part gives Yohko an apprentice   
  named Azusa and is even sillier than the 1st part.  Part 3 is    
  about a man Yohko falls in love in...in her dreams.  Part 5 has  
  been billed to be about the "death of Yohko"...
