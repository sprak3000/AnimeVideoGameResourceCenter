Kyatto Ninden Teyandee

  • Genre: Platform game
    Controls
  • A button: Jump
  • B button: Attack
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]
Pressing Start will pause the game and let you choose which of the three characters you want to play as. Special weapons can be picked up along the way; to use them, press Up + A. Tapping the directional pad twice and then holding it will make you run.
