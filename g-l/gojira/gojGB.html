GB Godzilla

  • Genre: Platform game
    Controls
  • A button: Use Fire Breath
  • B button: Punch
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]
Guide Godzilla as he rampages and destroys the ships, etc. that shoot at him. While on land, press B + Left to perform a spinning tail attack. No other information available at this time.

Godzilla

  • Genre: Puzzle game
    Controls
  • A button: [not used]
  • B button: Punch
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]
You must guide a cute looking Godzilla (who looks remarkably similar to the main character from Bubble Bobble) from stage to stage. Each puzzle seems to consist of dodging the creatures chasing you while trying to break or position the rocks to solve the puzzle.
