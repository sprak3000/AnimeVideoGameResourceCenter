Description:

  Golgo 13 is an assassin for hire.  He is the best in that once   
  hired, he will kill the target no matter who it is.  It doesn't  
  matter how much protection is around the target.  Golgo 13 is    
  also a completely amoral man with few redeeming qualities.  In   
  this film, one of Golgo 13's targets is the son of a very power- 
  ful man...who sends everything he can after the assassin.  The   
  suspense level is very high throughout the film, though the      
  mood is very dark.  Computer graphics are also used a lot.
