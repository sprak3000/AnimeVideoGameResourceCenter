Anime Video Game Resource Center

Welcome! After the creation of the newsgroup rec.arts.anime.games, I decided to design a page that would provide the anime and video game communities with a useful collection of information and links. Some of this information does fall through the cracks and into the bit bucket. Well, I have recycled some of it into the Odds and Ends page. All of the information you will find here deals with video games spawned from anime or manga (with a few exceptions). If you have any information, links, or corrections, please email me. All information under heading Links and FAQs are property of the respective authors. If you find any problems with the links under those headings not working, let me know. Otherwise, contact the author of that site. Also, I am looking for help on filling in certain parts of information on this site; read the help wanted section for what I need and why it would be worth your effort to help out. If you don't like frames or handy navigation bars, go to the frameless version.

For a growing and more complete list of anime video games, check out the Anime Video Games List maintained by Ken Arromdee. Looking for a site dedicated to Dragonball Z games? Then look no further than Kaoshin's Planet. There is also a site for Ranma 1/2 games that is slowly growing but has more screen captures than I do. Another place for Ranma 1/2 game information and pics is Al's Ranma 1/2 Video Game Page. Another good site to visit for a nice mix of FAQs is Hyper Games World. A list of seiyuu (voice actors/actresses) that have done video game voices is also available. If you are looking for more information on games featuring Godzilla, Gamera, or Ultraman, visit Gorzilla's Kaiju Datapedia.
