Laserdisc-based Arcade Games (by Doug Cranston)

Remember laserdisc-based arcade games like Dragon's Lair? Well, as some people may know, Cliffhanger used footage from Castle of Cagliostro and Mystery of Mamo to the same effect. Escape from Cyber City for the CDI uses footage from the two Galaxy Express movies as did the old and obscure arcade game Galaxy 999. Bega's Battle by Data East used footage from Harmageddon.
