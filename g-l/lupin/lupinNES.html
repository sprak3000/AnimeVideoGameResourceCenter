Lupin 3rd

  • Genre: Platform game
    Controls
  • A button: Jump
  • B button: Fire
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [Not Used]
You have three lives represented by being able to play Lupin, Jigen, and Goemon. As you go along, you can collect power-ups by shooting suitcases. These items include ballons to help you across long jumps and bullet-proof vests.
