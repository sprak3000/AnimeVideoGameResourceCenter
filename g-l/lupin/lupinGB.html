SD Lupin 3rd

  • Genre: Puzzle game
    Controls
  • A button: Jump
  • B button: Raise Floor/Lower Floor
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: Item Inventory
You guide Lupin to find the treasure on the level. You must avoid Zenigata and other enemies; along the way you can find help from Jigen and Goemon.
