Doraemon Nara no Suke no YABOU

  • Company: Sega
  • Date Released: 29 April 1993
  • Price (in yen): 3500
  • Genre: Platform game
    Controls
  • A button: Shoot
  • B button: Jump
  • Start: Pause
Nara no Suke is a name, and YABOU means ambition. The name is a pun on the popular game Nobunaga's Ambition. This is why the second no is smaller than the first.
