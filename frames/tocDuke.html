Duke Nukem 3D Patches

In the old days, there was a cute platform game called Duke Nukem. Today, Duke is grown up and is rippin' apart Doom and Quake. You run, jump, shoot, and swim your way through hordes of aliens bent on world domination (don't they have anything better to do?). Since Duke Nukem 3D is still relatively new, there is only one patch for it. Priss Nukem 3D lets you blast away the aliens as Priss. This is a very good patch for Bubblegum Crisis fans as well as ordinary Duke Nukem 3D fans.
