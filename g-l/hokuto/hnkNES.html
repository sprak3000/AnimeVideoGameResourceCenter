Hokuto no Ken

  • Genre: Platform game
    Controls
  • A button: Punch
  • B button: Kick
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]

From Drew Webber:

To proceed to the next level, keep walking left until you find a doorway with a little girl standing in it (she will appear and then disappear). At this doorway, press A+B+Up. Keep doing this until you find the level boss.
