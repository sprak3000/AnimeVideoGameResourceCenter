Description:

  Shin Kazama has a bright future.  He is an up and coming pilot   
  for Yamato Air Lines and has a relationship with the boss's      
  daughter and, perhaps, is destined to head the airline someday.  
  All this changes when his friend Kanzaki gets him drunk, and he  
  signs a paper which enlists him in the Aslam Foreign Legion.     
  There are 3 ways to get out: to get enough kills to buy your way 
  out; to survive until your term is up; or escape.  This is a     
  gritty, action-oriented series with accurate depictions of       
  military aircraft and associated hardware.  Note that the movies 
  are edited compilations from the TV series.
