Cyborg 009

  • Company: Bec
  • Date Released: 1994
  • Price (in yen): 8800
  • Genre: Platform game
    Controls
  • A button: Use Special Power
  • B button: Jump
  • X button: Use Special Power
  • Y button: Shoot
  • L button: Change Character
  • R button: Change Character
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]
You get to choose three different characters before the start of each mission. During the mission, you can switch between the three characters and use their unique skills to overcome obstacles.
