Appleseed

    Controls
  • A button: Shoot/Throw grenade
  • B button: Jump
  • X button: Cycle through Weapons Inventory
  • Y button: Shoot/Throw grenade
  • L button: Aim Diagonally
  • R button: Aim Diagonally
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: Cycle through Weapons Inventory
This is a side-scrolling action game which allows you to control one of the two main characters from the anime. Some object must be shot in order to activate or deactivate traps. Jump to take objects floating in the air such as keys. The players do not have a visible health bar, but they begin to change to varying shades of red depending on the amount of damage they absorb and eventually die. Pressing L or R button + A or Y will shoot diagonally. Pressing Up + B will make you jump higher. -- from Gabriel Jarero

Dojji Danpei

    Controls
  • A button: Throw
  • B button: Throw
  • X button: [not used?]
  • Y button: Jump
  • L button: [not used?]
  • R button: Change Character
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: Pause
Dojji (Dodge) Danpei appears to be some sort of dodge ball game. I have never heard of the anime. Apparently, you are the leader of a dodge ball team that takes on rival teams. You play in a basketball-like court; crossing the mid-line is a violation. Jumping over the mid-line to throw the ball at your opponent is allowed though. There are also teamates along the sidelines that you can pass the ball to and can grab a ball before it goes out of bounds. This is a very unique game.

    Controls
  • A button: Magic Attack

  • X button: [not used]
  • Y button: Attack
  • L button: [not used]
  • R button: [not used]
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]
This is a horizontal scrolling platform game where you control a magical girl skilled in the ways of kendo. While you move about the level, a meter charges up that lets you unleash a stronger normal attack. Using your magical attack will use up part of your life meter. Bowls of rice will recharge your life meter while clocks will freeze the enemies on the screen. The US version was called Kendo Rage.

To select your starting stage, highlight Configuration on the title screen and press X, Y, A, B, X, Y, A, B, Start. -- from Jacob Poon

    Controls
  • A button: Select Weapon

  • Y button: Attack
  • L button: Fire Left Arm Rocket
  • R button: Fire Right Arm Rocket
  • Start: Pause

This is a horizontal scrolling platform game where you control the mighty Mazinger Z. Your arms can be used as rocket powered boomerangs. The game was an extremely brief (and somewhat wrong) version of the Mazinger Z anime from Mazinger Z's first mission to the death of Dr. Hell. Holding the attack button will block, providing at least an arm is attached to Mazinger's body. Up + Attack will use a weapon, providing enough energy is available. Pressing Left/Right twice quickly will run. -- from Poon Jacob Tin Hang
