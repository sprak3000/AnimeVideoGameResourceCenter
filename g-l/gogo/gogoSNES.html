Go! Go! Ackman 3

  • Company: Banpresto
  • Date Released: 1995
  • Genre: Platform game
    Controls
  • A button: Use Item
  • B button: Jump
  • X button: Bomb
  • Y button: Attack
  • L button: [not used]
  • R button: [not used]
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]
Pressing Down + B will make you slide. You can jump on top of the enemies which will stun them and make you pick them up. They can then be thrown as a weapon. Stores along the way also sell useful items.
