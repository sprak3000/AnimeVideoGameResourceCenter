Makeruna Makendou

  • Genre: Platform game
    Controls
  • A button: Magic Attack
  • B button: Jump
  • X button: [not used]
  • Y button: Attack
  • L button: [not used]
  • R button: [not used]
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]
You control a magical girl skilled in the ways of kendo. While you move about the level, a meter charges up that lets you unleash a stronger normal attack. Using your magical attack will use up part of your life meter. Bowls of rice will recharge your life meter while clocks will freeze the enemies on the screen. The US version was called Kendo Rage.

From Jacob Poon:

To select your starting stage, highlight Configuration on the title screen and press X, Y, A, B, X, Y, A, B, Start.
