Hi no Tori (MSX)

The title translates to "The Firebird" or "The Phoenix". It is not the same anime as the Gameboy game called "Fighbird". This anime was created by Tezuka Osamu who also created the anime Astro Boy. It is a vertical-scrolling shooter.
