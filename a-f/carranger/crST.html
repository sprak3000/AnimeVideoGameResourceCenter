You control one of the five Car Rangers. You go along defeating enemies and collecting parts. At certain points, there are platforms where you pushing Up will take you to a place to obtain items or parts. By punching vehicles and platforms with a question mark, you can collect parts as well as weapons. Haven't played it long enough, but there is a gauge that fills up when you collect 60 parts. I am assuming you get a huge power-up when you fill the entire gauge.
