Doom Patches

Ever wish you could blast away some bad guys in Doom using the Kamehameha? Ever wanted to blow away a demon using the palm gun on Priss's hardsuit? How about using the powers of the Sailor Senshi to fry the Spider demon? Well, some Doom fanatics have created patches that let you do just that. Two places to look for a more complete list of patches are Anime Add-ons for Games and The Anime Doom Page.
