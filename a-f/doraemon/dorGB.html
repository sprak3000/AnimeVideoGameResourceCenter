Doraemon

  • Genre: Platform game
    Controls
  • A button: Punch
  • B button: Jump
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: Raise/Lower Map
You start on in a Zelda-like playing field. You collect items and punch out any enemies that get in your way. You come across doors that can take you into either a horizontal or vertical scrolling mini-game.
