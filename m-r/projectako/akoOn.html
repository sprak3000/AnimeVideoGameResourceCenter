Stu's Wacky-Waves: A-ko Bashing

  • Genre: Platform game
Stu's Wacky-Waves: A-ko Bashing at Anime HyperGuiderequires the Shockwave plug-in for your web browser. The object of the game is to bump B-ko more times than she bumps you. Your health meter goes down the more you get bumped by B-ko and the other obstacles in your way. Efficient use of the gas and brake is the key to this game.
