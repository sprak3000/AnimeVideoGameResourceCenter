Super Dimension Fortress Macross

  • Company: Namco/Bandai
  • Date Released: Dec 1985
  • Price (in yen): 4800
  • Genre: Shooting game
    Controls
  • A button: Change Valkyrie Form
  • B button: Fire Gun
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: Fire Missles
You pilot your Valkyrie as you blast countless swarms of enemy ships. To change the form of your Valkyrie, press the A button + Up/Left/Right.
