Yuu Yuu Hakusho Tokubetuhen

This is a cinematic fighting game with beautiful graphics. Hold Down + A/B/X/Y to perform a ki attack. Hold Right + A/B/X/Y to punch. Hold Left + A/B/X/Y to defend. Hold Up + X to jump. Hold Up + A/B/Y to perform character-based attack. -- from Jacob Poon
