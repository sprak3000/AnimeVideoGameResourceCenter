Fighbird (VGB)

This is a vertical-scrolling, shoot-em-up game. I have never heard of this anime, but it is listed in Ken Arromdee's list. You collect various power-ups along the way. There are also two different characters you can choose. Some strange glitches seem to throw you into different parts of the screen which kills you quickly.
