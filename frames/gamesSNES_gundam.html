Gundam Wing: Endless Duel

    Controls
  • A button: Punch
  • B button: Punch
  • X button: Punch
  • Y button: Kick
  • L button: Special Attack (with another button)
  • R button: Special Attack (with another button)
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]
This is a Street Fighter clone. You can choose from six different Gundam mechas to battle your way through a story mode or a VS mode. Pressing Forward, Forward will cause you to dash except for Deathscythe who telports. Pressing Back, Back will cause you to dash backwards without blocking. Pressing Forward + two buttons will dash forward while blocking. Pressing Back + two buttons will dash back while blocking. To super jump, press Up + two buttons. During the first super jump, you can super jump again by pressing Up (any direction) + two buttons. An air dash can be performed by pressing Forward + two buttons or Back + two buttons. Hold Down to perform a slow landing. -- from Jacob Poon
