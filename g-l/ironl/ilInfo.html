Description:

  Imagine the ultimate in sports teams...teams of robots that take 
  on almost any sport from baseball or football to ice hockey. The 
  games that these teams play become close to all out battles as   
  the primary goal is to win.  The focus of the series is on an    
  underequipped and underfunded team called the "Silver Castle".   
  The owner is a young and inexperienced girl.  Their nemesis is a 
  team called "Dark Kingdom" owned by a sinister and very rich man.
  The series follows the Silver Castle team, led by the baseball-  
  specialty robot Magmum Ace, as they go from game to game and     
  the obstacles that they have to overcome.  A mix between an      
  shounen action series and a sports series, "Iron Leaguer" works  
  on both levels.  An unusual touch is that the robots all have    
  "super-deformed" designs as in "SD Gundam".  A fun action series.
