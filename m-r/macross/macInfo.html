Description:

  An alien spaceship crashes on Macross Island, and Earth has to   
  deal with the alien fleet which is following it.  However,       
  human civilization is an example of 'protoculture' which the     
  Zentraedi (the aliens) are suppose to avoid.  Dubbed and         
  marketed as a segment of the 'Robotech' series.
