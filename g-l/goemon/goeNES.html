Ganbare Goemon

  • Genre: Action RPG
    Controls
  • A button: Jump
  • B button: Attack
  • Start: Pause
  • Select: [not used]
Jumping over pots and rocks releases power-up items. There are also geisha girls that run around occasionally; catching one gives you bonus points.
