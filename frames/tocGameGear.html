Sega Game Gear

In 1990, Sega came out with their own hand-held gaming system. The Game Gear featured color something the Gameboy was sorely lacking. The Game Gear was larger than the Gameboy overall, but it also gave you the ability to play some Master System games. The Game Gear did have a languauge lockout of a sort. A hardware switch was needed to change between english and japanese language games. You could play most of the games regardless of having the switch without any problems though.
